It watches your files and restarts the server automatically when you save.

Local Development	
    $ npm run watch (with nodemon) Auto-restarts on save.
Production Server	
    $ npm start

Nodemon starts your app on port 3000.

BrowserSync creates a "mirror" on port 4000.

You use localhost:4000 in your browser.

When you save a .pug or .js file, BrowserSync detects the change and automatically forces your browser to refresh.
